## 🔧 Print & Assembly Guidelines

* **Impact Resistance**: Steering components are highly prone to impact during front-end collisions. Print these using tough materials like **PETG**, **ABS**, or **ASA**. Avoid standard PLA, which is too brittle and will snap.
* **Slicing Settings**:
  * **Infill**: 40-50% Infill (Gyroid or Cubic pattern).
  * **Perimeters/Walls**: 4-5 walls (this provides the main structural strength).
* **Hardware Assembly**:
  * Use high-quality steel M3 screws and locknuts (nyloc nuts) to prevent the pivot joints from coming loose under vibration.
  * Do not over-tighten the screws on the pivot wings; the control arms should rotate smoothly under their own weight.
